# musictergym
This tool imports Spotify playlist CSV files to discover and download other music that matches your taste.

1. export your playlist CSV from https://exportify.net
2. click 'csv' button to import the playlist CSV.
3. click 'DOWN' button to select your folder for downloading.
4. S is similarity. A is Adventure. 
  S 90, A 30: Selects music that has a 90% similarity to your taste and a low adventure index of 30%.
  S 10, A 90: Selects music that has a 10% similarity to your taste and a high adventure index of 90%.
  The Adventure Index is determined by the track's view count (popularity).
